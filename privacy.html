How Zdravo Protects Your Privacy

Zdravo is built with privacy as its foundation. Unlike traditional chat apps that require accounts and store your data on servers, Zdravo works entirely through direct peer-to-peer connections using Bluetooth.

üö´ What We DON'T Collect

Zdravo never asks for or stores:

  • Your real name or email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your location data
  • Your contact list
  • Any personal information

üì± What Stays on Your Device

The only data Zdravo stores is kept locally on your device:

  • Your chosen nickname (like "CoffeeNinja")
  • A cryptographic key (for secure messaging)
  • Message history (only if you enable it, and only locally)

üîê Complete Control

Want to completely wipe all data? Triple-tap the Zdravo logo in the app. Everything disappears instantly - because it was never anywhere except your device.

üîí Security & Encryption

All messages are encrypted end-to-end using industry-standard cryptography. Even if someone could intercept your Bluetooth connection (which is already difficult), they'd only see encrypted data.

üë∂ Children's Privacy

Since Zdravo doesn't collect any personal information from anyone, it doesn't collect information from children either. There are no accounts to create and no data to worry about.

üì° Bluetooth Permission

Zdravo requires Bluetooth permission to connect with nearby devices. This permission is used solely for peer-to-peer communication and is never used for tracking or location monitoring.

üåê No Servers, No Problem

Because Zdravo has no servers, there's nothing to hack, no data breaches to worry about, and no company database with your information. Your conversations exist only between you and the people you're talking to.
